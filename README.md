# Customer-Churn-Project

I am done this project as a Data Scientist Intern at Intellipaat Software Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

Problem Statement:- As a Data Scientist at a telecom company “Leo” whose customers are churning out to its competitors. You have to analyse the data of your company and find insights and stop your customers from churning out to other telecom companies.

Domain:– Telecom Industry

Domain Context:– Customer churn, in simple terms means that the customer has stopped doing business with the company and this is a common problem when it comes to telecom industries. To avoid this, companies use predictive analysis to gauge the factors responsible for a customer to leave the company. These churn prediction models help in finding out the customer base that are most likely to churn out.
